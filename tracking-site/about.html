About RVS Logistics

Who We Are

RVS Logistics is a professional freight and delivery company, committed to making shipment tracking simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone. Founded in 2020, we've grown to become one of the most trusted names in logistics and parcel delivery across Europe and beyond.

Our platform connects customers with their parcels in real-time, providing accurate tracking information and estimated delivery times. With our own fleet of vehicles and dedicated drivers, we ensure complete control over your package from pickup to delivery.
